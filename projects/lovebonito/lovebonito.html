Love Bonito

The Proposal

Love Bonito had the vision to expand their clothing business to appeal to the international market. We discussed a clean, chic aesthetic and an easy-to-navigate approach that goes back to basics, allowing the products to shine.

Key Objective

The website should enable international users to have an improved experience to view the apparel available from the website.

Solution

To achieve this, we focused on creating a clean, minimalist design that highlighted the brand's chic aesthetic. By implementing a user-friendly navigation system and showcasing products through high-quality images, we aimed to provide an effortless shopping experience for international customers. Additionally, we integrated seamless international shipping options to cater to a diverse global audience.
