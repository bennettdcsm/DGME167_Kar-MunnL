Chuck's 

The Proposal

As part of the launching of their new physical store, Chuck’s wanted to update the good news on their website and introduce a reward system of their customer. They also took the opportunity to redesign the site to appeal to the younger audience.

Key Objective

The website should enable younger audience to have an improved experience to view the apparel available from the website.

Solution

Improved information architecture. Conducting surveys with young users and conducting user testing. Large image sizes for accessibility.
