Plan A Production 

The Proposal

Plan A Production has seen a boost inn clientele and seek a collaboration to revamp and improve the information architecture for their clients viewing.

Key Objective

The website should enable users to have an improved experience to view the services available from the website

Solution

To enhance Plan A Production's website, I conducted a comprehensive analysis of their existing information architecture. By streamlining the navigation, optimizing search functionality, and reorganizing content, I created a more user-friendly experience. The website was also redesigned with a modern, responsive layout to cater to diverse devices and improve overall user engagement.
